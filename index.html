Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Bengaluru, Shiro is an exquisite dining destination that transports you to the mystical Far East. With its stunning architecture, serene ambiance, and tantalizing flavors, Shiro offers a culinary journey through the diverse cuisines of Asia.

Our team of master chefs craft each dish with passion and precision, using only the finest ingredients to create authentic yet innovative interpretations of classic Asian favorites. From delicate dim sum to robust curries, from hand-rolled sushi to sizzling teppanyaki, our menu celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Japan, China, Thailand, and beyond.

At Shiro, dining is not just about food—it's an experience that engages all your senses and creates memories that linger long after the meal is over.
